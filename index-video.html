100% Accuracy with CAD/CAM Technology and 3D Printers

The shift away from traditional methods to a digital platform has implemented almost the entire Removable Partial Frameworks process to a digital base, including scanning, designing, printing and casting. Highly trained technicians use specialized software to expertly and carefully design a framework that is specific to the patient for a perfect fit. Features such as surveying and blocking out undercuts, and determining path of insertion allows for the design process to be extremely accurate and precise.

Top grade material

Digi32 Labs uses Vitallium 2000 to cast our partial denture frameworks. Its lightweight quality improves patient comfort and usability. Provides flexibility without deformation or fracture, which allows for chair-side adjustability. Greatly adjustable, yet extremely strong in order to withstand masticatory forces.

Consistent quality and precision

Traditional industry methods of designing Removable Partial Frameworks by hand do not allow for a precise fit every single time. Exocad software allows for pre-set preferences of clasp size, connector thickness and more, which are customized to the patient's needs. This allows for consistency of quality and fit that traditional methods lack.

Amazing Value

Digi32 uses high quality yet affordable prices for printing and casting. New digital approaches allows for a less time consuming and labour intensive manufacturing process, yet a high quality product. Smoother process creates a cost effective, reasonable price for consumers.
